Maritime flag stickers

An illustration of the phonetic alphabet featured on our project {rabbit waves}, drawn by Rek with ink and coloring pencils(see below photo of the original sketchbook drawing).

The illustrations are viewable on the International Maritime Flag Signals page. Initially, we made flag stickers so we could stick one on our wall near our radio as a reminder, but we thought it might serve other boaters well too(or fans of the phonetic alphabet)!

Buying a sticker is a good way to support our work on Rabbit Waves, too!
