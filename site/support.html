a studio on a sailboat

The idea of a nomadic design studio, we decided, was the best way to stay creative.

We enjoy the challenges that come with living off-grid. The power limitations and reduced connectivity has re-shaped how we work — in a good way. We now create our own applications, an open-source ecosystem of creative tools. We document our experience in the form of videos, and blog posts.

All the donations we get are re-invested back into the Hundredrabbits projects. Much of our tools are free, we'd like to keep it that way. Your support helps make that possible. There are many ways you can do that...
