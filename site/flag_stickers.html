Maritime flag stickers

An illustration of the phonetic alphabet featured on our project rabbit waves, drawn by Rek. The illustrations are viewable on the International Maritime Flag Signals page. Initially, we made flag stickers so we could stick one on our wall near our radio as a reminder, but we thought it might serve other boaters well too(or fans of the phonetic alphabet)!

Buying a sticker is a good way to support our work on Rabbit Waves too.

These stickers are:

  • A 10x15 cm (4x6 in) vinyl sticker sheet
  • Weather/UV resistant
  • Scratchproof
  • Each page is $14.00 CAD<(~$10 USD) plus shipping

To purchase stickers, select your shipping destination:

Canada United States International

The buttons will take you to a payment page, where it is possible to purchase more than 1 sheet, if desired.
